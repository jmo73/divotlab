Alignment Stick Gate
15 min · 20 balls · Accuracy
Place two alignment sticks 10 yards out, 6 feet apart. Every shot must land between them. Narrow the gate each session.
The 80% Swing
10 min · 15 balls · Tempo
Hit shots at 80% effort. Most amateurs gain accuracy and lose almost no distance by swinging within themselves.
3-2-1 Pressure Drill
10 min · 6 balls · Performance
Visualize a tight fairway. Hit 3 to target, then 2, then 1 final money ball. Score: 3pts fairway, 1pt rough.

10 min · 15 balls · Balance
