About Divot Lab

Golf, decoded.

Where the data meets the game.

What We Do

Divot Lab analyzes golf through data. Strokes gained. Tournament performance. Equipment. What actually matters for players at every level.

Why It Exists

Golf coverage tends toward hot takes or feel-good narratives. We focus on what the numbers show. Why certain players dominate. What separates skill levels. Whether equipment changes make a measurable difference.

Built for golfers who want to understand the game beyond the surface.

Our Process

Start with a question. Pull the relevant data from PGA Tour stats, DataGolf, USGA research, and other sources. Follow where it leads.

If the data contradicts conventional wisdom, we go with the data.

The Shop

Clean apparel. Quality materials. Subtle branding. For golfers who care about both the game and what they wear.

Who's Behind This

Divot Lab is a one-person project built by a golfer who got tired of hot takes passing as analysis. The goal was simple: take the same data the pros and their teams use, and make it accessible to everyone else.

Every article, every tool, every stat on this site is the product of genuine curiosity about what makes golfers better — filtered through real numbers rather than conventional wisdom. If the data says something surprising, we go with the data.
