How the picks are built.

01
Course-fit model
Every player in the field is scored against historical strokes gained data, weighted for what actually wins at this specific venue. Approach accuracy, driving distance, short game, and putting are weighted differently for every course.
02
Odds comparison
DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Pinnacle, Bet365, and Caesars are compared before every pick. The best available line is flagged. The difference between books can be meaningful over a full season.
03
Edge identification
When the model's implied probability is meaningfully higher than the book's implied probability, that gap is the bet. Not every pick wins — but consistently finding and betting positive expected value is how the ROI compounds.
