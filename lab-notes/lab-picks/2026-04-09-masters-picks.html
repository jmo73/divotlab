THIS WEEK'S APPROACH
Augusta National weights around-the-green play at 25% — the highest of any course in the model. The market prices the Masters like a pure approach/power event, but the data is clear: the players who rack up strokes around those greens are systematically undervalued. This week's card targets that gap, with two top-10 values whose ARG numbers the odds haven't fully priced in, a course-fit monster who remains a heavy favorite for good reason, and a former champion whose entire profile was built for this place.
