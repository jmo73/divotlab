What the score means
0–100. The top-scoring player in the field gets 100. The bottom gets 0. Everyone else is normalized between them. A score of 80+ means the player's skills strongly match this course's demands.

How it's calculated
Four strokes gained categories (OTT, Approach, Around the Green, Putting) are weighted based on what historically wins at this venue. Scores blend 24-month skill with 12-month recent form: 65% long-term, 35% recent.
